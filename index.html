About
After six

After Six was formed in 2022 by Dhika Pramudhia and Jonathan Edward, two passionate musicians from Jakarta, Indonesia. The name "After Six" reflects their creative peak time, often after 6 PM. Dhika, the vocalist and producer, and Jonathan, the guitarist, combine their talents to create a unique sound influenced by bands like Blink-182, BMTH, and Reality Club. Their debut song "Maybe" was released in June 2022, followed by "Bye Bye Beautiful Lady" on December 31, 2023, and their latest single, "For Your Dreams," on July 7, 2024. With dedication and a clear vision, After Six continues to create music that resonates with a wide audience.
